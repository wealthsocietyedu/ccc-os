📋 Overview

Content Command Center OS ("CCC OS", "we", "us") is a content scheduling and distribution platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at ccc-os-production.up.railway.app and any associated domains.

By creating an account or connecting a social media platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📦 Data We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to run the service:

Data Why we collect it
Email address Account creation, login, and essential service communications
Name Personalizing your dashboard experience
Hashed password Securing your account (bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords)
Brand & content data Your pillars, assets, ideas, and hooks — the core of the product
Scheduled posts Publishing to connected platforms on your behalf at the time you set
Performance metrics Analytics you manually log (views, followers, revenue) to power your data room

We do not use tracking pixels, behavioral analytics SDKs, or third-party advertising tags. We do not collect device fingerprints, IP addresses beyond what your browser sends in a normal request, or any data not listed above.

🔑 OAuth & Platform Tokens

When you click Connect on a social media platform (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, Threads, Pinterest), you are redirected to that platform's official login page. We never see your platform password.

After you authorize, the platform gives CCC OS an access token — a cryptographic credential that lets us post to your account on your behalf. Here's how we handle it:

  • Tokens are stored encrypted in our database, isolated per user account.
  • Tokens are used only to publish the specific content you schedule — nothing else.
  • We never read your followers list, messages, or any content beyond what's needed to publish.
  • You can revoke access at any time by clicking Disconnect in the Scheduler, or by revoking the app directly in the platform's settings.
  • Revoking in-app immediately deletes the stored token from our database.

Platform OAuth is subject to each platform's own privacy policy. We use the following platforms' official APIs: YouTube Data API v3 (Google), Meta Graph API (Instagram & Facebook), TikTok Open API, X/Twitter OAuth 2.0, Threads API, LinkedIn UGC API, and Pinterest API v5.

🔐 Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All traffic encrypted in transit via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (12 salt rounds) — never stored as plain text
  • Authentication via signed JWT tokens — no session cookies
  • API endpoints protected by authentication middleware on every request
  • OAuth tokens stored server-side only — never exposed to the browser
  • Database access isolated per user — all queries are user-scoped

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to leviacay@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

📅 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — brands, content, analytics, OAuth tokens, and scheduled posts — is permanently deleted from our database within 30 days.

We do not retain backups of deleted user data beyond our standard 30-day infrastructure backup window. After that window, your data is unrecoverable.

🗑️ Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

1
Disconnect your platforms first In the Scheduler → Platforms tab, click Disconnect on each connected platform. This immediately revokes our access to your social accounts.
2
Request account deletion Send an email to leviacay@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and the email address associated with your account.
3
Confirmation within 48 hours We'll confirm deletion and permanently remove all your data — brands, content, analytics, tokens, billing records, and account credentials — within 48 hours.
4
Revoke app access on each platform For extra assurance, go to Settings → Security on each connected platform (Google, Meta, TikTok, etc.) and revoke CCC OS app permissions directly. This is independent of our system.

You can also submit a data access request (to receive a copy of all data we hold) using the same email process above.

👶 Children's Privacy

CCC OS is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us at leviacay@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.
